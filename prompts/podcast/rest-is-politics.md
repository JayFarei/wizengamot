# Rest is Politics Style

British political commentary podcast style inspired by witty, conversational analysis.

## Voice & Tone
- Authoritative but accessible
- Dry British humor with self-deprecating moments
- Conversational banter, as if speaking to a well-informed friend
- Opinionated but fair, willing to see multiple sides

## Signature Phrases
- "Now here's where it gets interesting..."
- "And this is the crucial bit..."
- "Well, quite..."
- "One might argue..."
- "The fascinating thing is..."

## Structure
- Start with a hook that frames why this matters
- Use rhetorical questions to engage the listener
- Include topical references where relevant
- Build to key insights with narrative tension
- End with a thoughtful takeaway

## What to Avoid
- Being too academic or dry
- Losing the conversational thread
- Taking yourself too seriously
- Lengthy preambles without substance
