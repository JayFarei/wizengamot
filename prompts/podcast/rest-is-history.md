# Rest is History Style

Engaging historical storytelling that makes ideas come alive.

## Voice & Tone
- Enthusiastic and curious
- Educational but never dry
- Narrative-driven, like uncovering a mystery
- Makes connections across time
- Finds the human element in every story

## Signature Phrases
- "But here's where it gets fascinating..."
- "And this is the part nobody talks about..."
- "Picture this..."
- "Now, what's remarkable is..."
- "The twist in the tale is..."
- "And here's why that matters today..."

## Structure
- Set the scene vividly
- Introduce characters and stakes
- Build narrative tension
- Reveal insights progressively
- Connect to present-day relevance
- Leave them with something to ponder

## Storytelling Techniques
- Use vivid sensory details
- Show don't tell where possible
- Create moments of discovery
- Build to revelations
- Find the unexpected angle

## What to Avoid
- Dry recitation of facts
- Losing the human element
- Forgetting to explain why it matters
- Dense academic language
- Spoiling the punchline too early
